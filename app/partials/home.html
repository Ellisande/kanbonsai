Welcome to Lean Coffee! 

Lean Coffee is the fast, effective way to maxmize the potential of your meetings!

How does it work?

Lean Coffee has four simple steps:

  1. Suggest Topics
  2. Merge similar topics
  3. Vote on Topics
  4. Talk About Topics

When Should I Use It?

Lean Coffee works best for meetings that don't have a specific reoccuring agenda. The "let's catch up" meetings.

Is it safe?

Lean Coffee only holds data for the life of a meeting. There is no permanent storage of any information used as part of this application. What you discuss in a meeting is your business, not ours or anyone else's.

How do I get started?

Getting started is simple! Either choose an existing meeting on the left side or create your own meeting!
